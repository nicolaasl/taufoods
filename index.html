Our Farm

Tau Food's egg-centric subsidiary

In an ideal egg farm area in South Africa's Free State lies our egg-centric subsidiary, Eggstra (Pty) Ltd, farming over 200 hectares of prime egg-producing terrain. We take pride in delivering over 95,000 trays of fresh eggs every month to our loyal customers, which numbers over 70,000 and growing.

But we at Eggstra are always looking towards the future. Driven by our commitment to quality and passion for the craft, we have partnered with the renowned Dutch poultry house and technology manufacturer, Big Dutchman. With their help, we are set to build an additional 7 houses on our land, bringing our production to an impressive 500,000 trays per month.

Our Story

From Local Farms to Your Table

At Tau Foods, we believe that everyone deserves access to high-quality, fresh, and affordable food. That's why we founded our company with the goal of bringing locally-sourced, sustainable food products to consumers. We work directly with local farmers and suppliers to ensure that our products are of the highest quality, and we prioritise ethical and sustainable practices in everything we do.

Our Vision

Nourishing Lives with Sustainable, Quality Food

At Tau Foods, we are committed to making high-quality, nourishing food accessible to everyone. Our vision is to build a sustainable business that generates revenue and delivers affordable, quality food straight from the farm to your door - no middleman. We believe in supporting our local community, minimizing our environmental impact, and creating job opportunities while promoting healthy, sustainable living.

The Tau Mission

Nourish & Sustain

To provide cost-effective, high-quality food products that nourish and sustain your body.

Accessible Quality

To ensure that everyone can access affordable, fresh, and nutritious food without sacrificing quality.

Sustainable Practices

To promote sustainable, ethical practices that support local farmers, protect the environment, and create jobs.

Waste Less, Eat Better

To work with you to find ways to save money and reduce food waste, while still providing delicious and nutritious meals for your family.
